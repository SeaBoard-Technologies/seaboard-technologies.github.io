HR Specialist

  • Position: Human Resources and Recruitment Specialist for a Seed-Stage Corporate Venture
  • Reports to: CEO/Founders
  • Load: Part-time or full-time
  • Location: Singapore, India or remote

We are seeking an experienced, motivated and hands-on HR and recruitment specialist to guide and coordinate the team building efforts in our seed-stage corporate venture.

Having recently launched a pilot product and secured funding, we are looking to build our in-house product and corporate team. For this, we want to grow from less than 10 staff to 20+ in the next year mostly in Southeast and South Asia.

Besides recruiting, we also need to shape the culture, policies and overall ways of working.

What will you be doing?

You will be responsible to help the founding team with recruitment and advise, plan and implement HR governance and culture-shaping efforts:

  1. Recruiting: Manage and guide the entire process from finding suitable talent to hiring and ensure a great recruitment experience
  2. Employee Lifecycle: Support employees from onboarding to off-boarding
  3. Team Building: Support building and maintaining the culture for high-performing teams
  4. People Growth: Support employees in their professional growth
  5. Policy Tracking: Know and understand employment laws and regulations and set internal policies in line with these

What should you bring with you?

To succeed, you should have the following experiences and skills:

  1. At least 5 years experience in technology recruiting within a fast-paced technology, innovation or consulting company or head-hunting firm
  2. Passion for building successful, diverse and healthy teams with people thriving and growing in order to have a positive impact on the world we inhabit and shape
  3. You know what good talent looks like, what it is worth and how to keep talented people happy
  4. Independence, adaptable, self-driven and people-centric and a great influencer to build trust, guide stakeholders and set standards
  5. Expertise with technology profiles and leadership roles and the ability to cope with high volume hiring if needed
  6. Solid working knowledge with recruitment and online collaboration tools; good English fluency
  7. Ideally, an educational background in a relevant field and experience with data analytics
