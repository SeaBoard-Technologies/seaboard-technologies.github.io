Chief Executive Officer

  • Position: Chief Executive Officer (CEO) for a Marine Fintech Corporate Venture
  • Reports to: Board of Directors and investors
  • Load: Full-time
  • Location: Singapore

We are seeking an experienced, motivated and hands-on CEO to lead our seed-stage corporate venture.

Having recently closed seed funding and launched an internal pilot, we are looking to move the product to an MVP stage, which can scale up on number of users and functionalities while also shaping the foundation of the business building the team, defining culture and developing the strategic roadmap.

What will you be doing?

You will be responsible to work with the founding team and stakeholders to lead the business strategically and on a day-to-day basis:

  1. Corporate Strategy: Developing the company’s overall strategy and vision, and working with the whole team to implement these

  2. Financial Viability: Securing financial stability and success

  3. Team Building: Growing, managing and setting a positive example for high-performing teams across product, sales and marketing, customer service and corporate operations

  4. Sales & Customer Success: Grow the potential customer base, conversion rate and customer success by driving transformation in the maritime space

  5. Stakeholder Management: Represent and report to investors and stakeholders

  6. Ecosystem Navigation: Developing partnerships and relationships with key stakeholders

What should you bring with you?

To succeed, you should have the following experiences and skills:

  1. Experience leading successful organisations, startups or significant teams with P/L in the fintech, maritime or commerce space

  2. Excellent communication skills with the ability to create clarity and share this understanding across diverse teams and settings

  3. Experience building and leading successful, diverse and healthy teams doing agile work with a good culture of collaboration

  4. Strong analytical and strategic capabilities with a strong understanding of the market trends and customer needs

  5. Passion for creating innovative solutions that have a positive impact on the world we inhabit and shape

  6. Minimum a bachelor’s degree in a relevant field and an MBA combined with 15–20 years of experience in the finance, maritime or technology space; prior experience in operations, finance, corporate development is desirable
