Product (UX) Designer

  • Position: Product (UX) Designer for a Marine Fintech Corporate Venture
  • Reports to: Head of Product
  • Load: Full-time
  • Location: India or Singapore

We are seeking an experienced, motivated and hands-on product and UX designer to help bringing our seed-stage corporate venture product (and company) from pilot stage to MVP.

Having recently launched a pilot product and secured funding, we are looking to build our product team creating meaningful, delightful and valuable products to be launched to a global audience.

Besides shaping the products, we also are building our organisation, its brand and culture, which need to value great design equally to great engineering.

What will you be doing?

You will be responsible for designing our digital products for end-consumers, business users and internal teams. As part of our design team, you will be doing:

  1. User Research: Design, conduct and analyse user research studies before and after the product development
  2. Product Design: Design digital products for multiple user groups across different digital channels; products that are useful, usable and delightful for their users
  3. Product Development: Deliver great products with cross-functional teams by focussing on quickly creating solid understanding, alignment and clarity on collaborative, elegant and creative solutions; test the products built and help the team to deliver quality
  4. Team Building: Grow the quality of the design team by collaboration with fellow designers through review and feedback sessions; create reusable patterns, style guides and best practices for an iterative and efficient design process
  5. Brand & Culture: Help developing and defining the SeaBoard product and company brand by being a champion for great design as a value; collaborate with externals experts as necessary

You will be at the core of the growing design and product team and should enjoy the opportunity to have impact and shape.
