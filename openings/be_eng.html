Back-End Engineer

  • Position: Back-End Engineer for a Marine Fintech Corporate Venture
  • Reports to: CTO/Engineering Lead
  • Load: Full-time
  • Location: India or Singapore

We are seeking an experienced, keen and impactful Backend Engineer to architect, build and maintain the core systems allowing our product to grow from pilot to MVP.

You will be responsible for designing and building the architecture of our platform's backend systems, implementing and maintaining our APIs, databases, and servers, and ensuring the platform's security and compliance with relevant regulations. You will also collaborate with frontend developers to ensure seamless integration of the backend and frontend systems, and participate in agile development process and contribute to product development decisions.

What will you be doing?

You will be working as part of the core tech team to build the technical architecture of product and infrastructure to develop standards with the technical lead:

  1. Technical Architecture & Governance: Architect, implement, configure, test and maintain product infrastructure, backend system APIs, databases
  2. Product Development: Participate and drive the development and troubleshooting of the core product; collaborate with front-end, design and business to build efficient and scalable systems; develop secure backend systems following best practices and standards (PCI-DSS, GDPR)
  3. Deployment & Operations: Setup and maintain CI/CD processes, monitor, troubleshoot and optimise live systems; Manage system security and compliance though audits and configuration as per best practices
  4. Team Building & People Growth: Help growing the backend team; collaborate with fellow engineers to deliver better work and develop best practices and a our shared methodology
