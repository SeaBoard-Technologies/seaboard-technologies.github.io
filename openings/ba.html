Back-End Engineer

  • Position: Business Analyst for a Marine Fintech Corporate Venture
  • Reports to: Head of Product
  • Load: Full-time
  • Location: India or Singapore

We are seeking a dedicated, keen and impactful Business Analyst to support the product and engineering teams in growing our product from pilot to MVP.

You will be responsible for understanding and communicating the user and business needs in detail and supporting the Product Owners and Engineering Leads in building alignment and sharing this understanding across the teams. You will be using tools like process maps, data models and object lifecycle maps to create a shared understanding in the whole team. As the expert on the nitty-gritty details of the product, you are essential for the team to build meaningful and valuable products.

What will you be doing?

You will be working as part of the product team to understand business and user needs, analyse them and own the logical structure of the product. Your attention to detail and insights will allow the teams to focus on building value:

  1. User Research & Success: Understand user and business needs and track their achievement by analysing, mapping and validating the business and user needs and processes; help with user onboarding and support, as well as product adoption monitoring to gauge user satisfaction, gaps and opportunities
  2. Product Design: Deeply understand the problem space, build clarity and define solutions fitting the constraints; experiment with tools and prototypes to evaluate evaluate their suitability; think through the product and how it should work in the main and edge cases, define their logical structure using patterns and converting these into detailed technical requirements
  3. Product Development: Support and align the product team to support the successful delivery of meaningful products: support product owners to prioritise and risk manage projects, support designers and developers to ensure the product fits all the requirements and support QA in validating that the product delivers all that the users and business needs
  4. Deployment and Operations: Continuously monitor and analyse the operations and performance of the product to identify opportunities for improvement

What should you bring with you?

To succeed, you should have the following experiences and skills:

  1. Bachelor's or ideally Master's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, Engineering or similar with 3+ years of work experience. Work experience as a developer and knowledge of architectural frameworks is beneficial, but not required.
  2. Good fluency in English, ability to communicate clearly and ability to guide a conversation in order to achieve alignment in cross-functional teams
  3. Analytical skills to make the right decisions based on the right informed criteria (data, intuition, etc.) and problem-solving skills to think creatively and come up with innovative solutions
  4. Curiosity, a knack for taking things apart and putting them together again and fearlessness about new tools and technologies and an eagerness to dive in, experiment and learn to figure out opportunities of use and challenges
  5. A deep care for building meaningful products that bring value to users and the business and the ability to convert this into clear technical writing
  6. Strive towards impact through awareness, flexibility, focus on achieving clarity in problem understanding, as well as diligent and efficient collaboration in working on the solution
