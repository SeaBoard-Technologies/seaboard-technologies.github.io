Legal and Tax Considerations

Do I need to declare this account for tax purposes?

If you are a Resident Indian (RI), which means you did not complete 183 days of sailing, you may need to declare your SeaBoard account for tax purposes. To do so, you will need to transfer the money from your SeaBoard account to your home country bank account and declare it to relevant authorities.

If you are a Non-Resident-Indian (NRI), which means you have completed 183 days of sailing, you may not need to declare the SeaBoard account. There is no limit to how much you can hold in your account. However, you may need to do so if you become a RI.

If you are unsure of your residential status, please consult with a chartered accountant (CA) or a tax specialist.

References: Income Tax Act, 1961, Section 6

DBS Bank of Singapore is the custodian of the money held in SeaBoard. Rated as one of the world's best and safest banks, your money is secure.

Is my SeaBoard account regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI)?

No. Since SeaBoard is a Singapore-registered company, the RBI's regulations or guidelines do not apply. As such, we are not required to share your transaction information with Indian authorities.

Since I don't qualify for Non-Resident Indian (NRI) status, how do I declare my SeaBoard account?

SeaBoard is a Singapore-based account / digital wallet. It is not regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). If you don't qualify for NRI status, you will need to transfer the money from your SeaBoard account to your home country bank account and declare it to relevant authorities.

References: Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), 1999

Fees

What are transaction fees?

You will be charged a fee for SeaBoard-to-bank transfers, online or offline purchases, and ATM withdrawals. The first SeaBoard-to-bank transfer (also known as remittance) each month is free. For more information on transaction fees, refer to the SeaBoard app.

Will I be charged a card maintenance fee?

No. There is no card maintenance fee.

Will I be charged an account maintenance fee?

No. There is no account maintenance fee.

Will I be charged for transferring/remitting money from my Seaboard account to my bank savings account?

The first remittance per received wage is free; subsequent payouts will incur fees.

Will I be charged a fee if I choose to allot all or a portion of my wages to my SeaBoard account?

No. There is no allotment fee.

Is there any minimum balance requirement?

No minimum balance is required.

Currently, you will not be able to schedule payments to beneficiaries. However, we are working on it and may be available in the near future.

Will I earn interest on the balance maintained in my SeaBoard account?

Since this is a digital account, there is no interest provided on the balance maintained.

How can I receive my wages in the Seaboard Account?

To receive wages in your SeaBoard account, you need to provide the following information to your ship captain:

  1. Your name
  2. Your SeaBoard account identifier (available in the app)
  3. Your Continuous Discharge Certificate (CDC) number

Your ship captain will pass this information to your employer's accounts team for wages allotment.

Account Setup

What documents do I need to provide to set up a SeaBoard account?

You will need to provide a copy of your Continuous Discharge Certificate (CDC) and passport to set up a SeaBoard account. Please ensure that the documents:

  1. Are clear; not cropped
  2. Contain the front and back pages
  3. Have not expired

How long does it take to set up a SeaBoard account and get a physical card?

Your SeaBoard account will take approximately 2 to 3 working days to be ready, as we will need to verify your documents for security purposes. Once your SeaBoard account is ready, we will issue your physical card within 2 working days. We will also keep you updated on your card issuance status in the app.

Will there be an account number? What can I use it for?

Each SeaBoard account has a unique alpha-numeric account identifier (example: SB 1234 567 890) which you can find in the app. You can share this account identifier with your employer, ship master or other SeaBoard app users to receive wages or money.
