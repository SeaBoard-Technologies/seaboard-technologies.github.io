Known Issues

We are committed to providing the best user experience possible. As we continue to improve the app, there are known limitations that we need time to resolve. Here is a list of limitations that you should be aware of.

Wallet

Limitation Versions Affected
Only USD can be held v0.6 onwards

Transfers

Limitation Versions Affected
Only USD can be sent to SeaBoard peers v0.6 onwards
Only China, India, Philippines and Vietnam bank accounts are supported v0.6 onwards

Cards

Limitation Versions Affected
Cash withdrawal in Singapore are not allowed by local regulations v0.6 onwards
Adding cards to Apple Pay is currently not supported\
(however, Google and Samsung Pay are supported!)		 v0.6 onwards
Physical card CVV and expiry date are not displayed in app due to security reasons | v0.6 onwards v0.6 onwards

Contact Management

Limitation Versions Affected
The app does not support real-time search. Recipients can only be found via full email address v0.6 onwards
Adding of a bank recipient requires recipient’s contact number, which currently only supports contact numbers in India (+91), Vietnam (+84), Philippines (+63), China (+86) v0.6 onwards
When making a transfer to a bank, some bank names may be truncated due to space limitations v0.6 onwards
